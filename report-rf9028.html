Findings

External corrosion observed on shell mid-section near support saddles. Paint coating shows visible blistering and minor flaking with surface rust uniformly distributed across the affected zone.

No localized pitting or wall-loss anomalies were detected during visual inspection. Coating thickness measurements suggest the original protection system has degraded but the underlying base metal remains within acceptable structural margins.

Short-term Recommendation

Surface preparation (SP-2 hand-tool cleaning) and touch-up coating at corroded zones within 30 days to halt further metal loss.

Long-term Recommendation

Full blast and re-coating of external surface during 2027 turnaround. Update paint cycle interval from 7 years to 5 years for this equipment class given environmental exposure.
